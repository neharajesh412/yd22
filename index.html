    We are not who we used to be
    When we wake up, we get to choose how we can help someone within our abilities and when we do, the grateful smile we'll receive is something precious. Yagna Dhruva '22, IMPEL ANEW LEGACY
    is not just arising but is also striving to fulfill the needs of lives in need. As this is the 12th Edition, we will donate a part of the fund that we raise to 12 acid attack survivors who have been through thick and thin to survive. Chaanv Foundation is a government approved NGO who have always been with the survivors every step of their way and we'd be honoured to be a part of them.
